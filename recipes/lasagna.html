Lasagna Soup Recipe

Description

Delicious lasagna soup with good flavoring and texture. Simple to make and low in carbs.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Tomatoes
  • Chicken or Beef stock
  • Green onions
  • Pasta
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Brown the meat
  2. Cook onion and garlic
  3. Add sauce and spices
  4. Add pasta
  5. Combine Cheese
