Overnight Oats Recipe

Description

Overnight oats are a no-cook, cold breakfast alternative where rolled oats are soaked in liquid (like milk) overnight, resulting in a creamy, porridge-like texture ready to eat in the morning. This method avoids cooking and allows for a quick, convenient breakfast that's often customized with various mix-ins like fruit, nuts, and seeds.

Ingredients

  • Rolled oats
  • Milk
  • Fruit of choice
  • Peanut butter
  • Chia seeds
  • Chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients
  2. Put all ingredients together in jar with milk
  3. Let sit overnight in fridge
