Eggs Benedict Recipe

Description

Eggs Benedict is a common American breakfast or brunch dish, consisting of two halves of an English muffin, each topped with Canadian bacon or sliced ham, a poached egg, and hollandaise sauce.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • English Muffins
  • Hollandaise Sauce
  • Bacon
  • Sliced ham

Steps

  1. Poach the eggs
  2. Make the hollandaise sauce
  3. Cut the green onions
  4. Fry the bacon
  5. Toast the english muffins and put everything together
